Purpose

"The Pohnpei Technology Chamber of Commerce fosters local innovation, collaboration, and sustainable growth. Our mission is to empower businesses and professionals through technology adoption, knowledge exchange, and advocacy for progressive policies. We aim to position Pohnpei as a hub for technological excellence, driving economic development for a prosperous future."
